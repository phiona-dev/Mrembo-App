## Problem
Many skincare users in Kenya struggle to determine whether skincare products are safe to use. Ingredient labels are often unclear, and some products contain harmful or banned ingredients that can cause skin irritation, bleaching, or long-term damage.

## Solution
Mrembo is a skincare safety app that allows users to check skincare products and view ingredient safety information. The app highlights harmful or banned ingredients to help users make safer skincare choices.

## MVP Features
- User authentication (login)
- Skincare product list
- Ingredient safety checker
- Warnings for harmful or banned ingredients
